## HEART

**Cardiovascular System — Your engine**

### Aerobic Capacity
*How much oxygen your body can use*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **VO2max** | Apple Watch (auto) · Garmin/Polar (auto) · Treadmill test (clinical) · Cooper test (self) | Maximum aerobic capacity — #1 predictor of lifespan |
| **Zone 2 Capacity** | Apple Watch workout mins · Strava/TrainingPeaks · Self-log (mins/week) | Sustainable aerobic base — foundation for all cardio fitness |

### Cardiac Efficiency
*How efficiently your heart works*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Resting Heart Rate** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) · Manual morning check | Heart efficiency at rest — lower is generally better |
| **Heart Rate Recovery** | Apple Watch post-workout · Manual (HR drop after 1-2 min) | How fast HR drops after exertion — cardiac fitness marker |
| **HRV** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) · Chest strap + app | Autonomic nervous system balance — recovery and stress indicator |
| **Max Heart Rate** | 220-age formula · Apple Watch max detected · All-out effort test | Cardiovascular ceiling — used to calculate training zones |

### Cardiovascular Health
*Clinical markers of heart disease risk*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Blood Pressure** | Home BP cuff · Pharmacy kiosk · Doctor visit · Some smartwatches | Vascular pressure — major CV risk factor when elevated |
| **ApoB** | Lab upload (bloodwork, request specifically) | Atherogenic particles — causal driver of heart disease |
| **Lp(a)** | Lab upload (bloodwork, one-time genetic test) | Genetic cardiovascular risk — test once, doesn't change |
| **LDL / HDL** | Lab upload (standard lipid panel) | Traditional cholesterol markers — part of CV risk picture |
| **CAC Score** | CT scan (cardiac imaging center) | Coronary artery calcium — actual plaque buildup in arteries |

## FRAME

**Musculoskeletal System — Your structure**

### Upper Body Strength
*Pushing and pulling capacity*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Pushing Strength** | Push-up max (self-test) · Bench press weight · Dumbbell press weight | Upper body pushing power — functional for daily activities |
| **Pulling Strength** | Pull-up max (self-test) · Row weight · Lat pulldown weight | Upper body pulling power — back strength and posture |

### Lower Body Strength
*Leg power and endurance*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Leg Strength** | 30-sec sit-to-stand (self-test) · Squat max weight · Leg press weight | Lower body power — predicts functional capacity and fall risk |
| **Leg Endurance** | Wall sit time · Squat hold time · Bodyweight squat reps | Lower body stamina — muscular endurance under load |

### Grip Strength
*Hand and forearm power*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Grip** | Dynamometer reading (kg) · Dead hang time · Farmer carry (weight × time) | Overall strength proxy — strongest mortality predictor for strength |

### Stability & Balance
*Postural control and fall prevention*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Balance** | Single-leg stand eyes closed · Single-leg stand eyes open · Balance board time | Postural stability — predicts fall risk and mortality in older adults |
| **Core Stability** | Plank hold time · Side plank time · Dead bug reps | Trunk control — foundation for all movement and injury prevention |

### Body Composition
*What your body is made of*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Body Fat %** | Photo AI analysis · Smart scale · DEXA scan · Calipers · Navy method | Fat mass relative to total — metabolic health indicator |
| **Lean Mass** | Smart scale estimate · DEXA scan · Photo AI progress | Muscle and bone mass — protective tissue for longevity |
| **Waist** | Photo AI analysis · Tape measure at navel | Visceral fat proxy — central obesity is high-risk |
| **Weight** | Apple Health (auto from scale) · Smart scale · Manual input | Total body mass — trend matters more than absolute number |
| **Waist-to-Hip Ratio** | Photo AI · Tape measure (calculated) | Fat distribution pattern — apple vs pear shape risk |

### Mobility & Flexibility
*Range of motion and movement quality*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Overall Mobility** | Video AI movement assessment · FMS screen (pro) · Self-assessment (1-10) | Functional range of motion — can you move well through full ROM? |
| **Hip Mobility** | Deep squat hold test · Video AI · 90/90 position test | Hip joint freedom — critical for lower body function |
| **Shoulder Mobility** | Reach-behind test · Video AI · Shoulder rotation test | Shoulder joint freedom — affects overhead and pulling movements |
| **Hamstring Flexibility** | Sit-and-reach test · Toe touch (photo) · Straight leg raise | Posterior chain length — affects back health and hip function |

### Structural Health
*Skeletal integrity and alignment*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Posture** | Photo AI side profile · Mirror self-check · Physio assessment | Spinal alignment — affects pain, breathing, and movement |
| **Joint Pain** | Self-report (0-10 per joint) · Pain questionnaire | Structural wear signal — early warning for intervention |
| **Bone Density** | DEXA scan (clinical) | Skeletal strength — fracture risk, especially important 40+ |

## MIND

**Cognitive System — Your clarity**

### Attention & Focus
*Ability to direct and sustain attention*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Focus Duration** | Focus timer apps (Forest, etc.) · iOS Focus Mode time · Self-estimate | Sustained attention span — how long before distraction |
| **Deep Work Hours** | Calendar analysis · Time tracker (Toggl, RescueTime) · Self-report | Uninterrupted cognitive work — high-value output time |
| **Flow States** | Self-report (count per week) · Flow journal | Peak focus experiences — when time disappears in work |

### Digital Behavior
*Relationship with devices and apps*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Screen Time** | iOS Screen Time (auto) · Android Digital Wellbeing (auto) | Total daily screen exposure — quantity of digital time |
| **Phone Pickups** | iOS Screen Time (auto) · Android Digital Wellbeing (auto) | Compulsive checking frequency — attention fragmentation |
| **Social Media Time** | iOS Screen Time by app · Android Digital Wellbeing | High-distraction app usage — dopamine-driven scrolling |
| **Notifications** | iOS Screen Time (auto) · Notification audit (manual) | External interruptions — forced attention breaks |

### Mental Practices
*Intentional cognitive training*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Mindful Minutes** | Apple Health Mindful Minutes (auto) · Headspace/Calm stats · Manual log | Time in meditation/breathwork — attention training |
| **Reading Time** | Kindle stats · Apple Books · Self-report (mins/day) | Deep, sustained reading — focused cognitive engagement |
| **Learning Time** | Course platform stats · Duolingo/etc. · Self-report | Active skill acquisition — brain plasticity maintenance |
| **Journaling** | Self-report (days/week) · Day One stats · Journal app | Reflective practice — self-awareness and processing |

### Stress & Emotional
*Mental and emotional state*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Perceived Stress** | Quick check-in (1-10) · PSS-10 questionnaire (validated) | Subjective stress load — how overwhelmed you feel |
| **Anxiety** | Quick check-in (1-10) · GAD-7 questionnaire (validated) | Worry and tension level — impacts focus and sleep |
| **Mood** | Quick check-in (1-10) · Mood tracker app · Daylio | Emotional state — affects motivation and decision-making |
| **Resilience** | Brief Resilience Scale (BRS questionnaire) | Bounce-back capacity — recovery from setbacks |

### Cognitive Performance
*Brain processing capabilities*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Reaction Time** | Online reaction test · Cognitive app · Gaming benchmark | Neural processing speed — how fast brain responds |
| **Working Memory** | N-back test app · Digit span test · Cambridge Brain Sciences | Mental holding capacity — juggling information in mind |
| **Processing Speed** | Cognitive test apps · Trail Making Test · Pattern matching | How fast you think — speed of cognitive operations |

## METABOLISM

**Metabolic System — Your fuel system**

### Glucose Regulation
*Blood sugar control and stability*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Fasting Glucose** | Lab upload (bloodwork) · Home glucose meter · CGM | Baseline blood sugar — early diabetes warning |
| **HbA1c** | Lab upload (bloodwork) | 3-month glucose average — gold standard for glucose control |
| **Glucose Variability** | CGM (Levels, Libre, Dexcom) | Blood sugar stability — spikes and crashes pattern |
| **Post-Meal Response** | CGM · Finger prick 1-2h after meals | How food affects blood sugar — glycemic impact |
| **Fasting Insulin** | Lab upload (bloodwork, request specifically) | Insulin baseline — often elevated before glucose rises |
| **HOMA-IR** | Calculated (from fasting glucose + insulin) | Insulin resistance score — metabolic dysfunction marker |

### Energy & Nutrition
*Fuel input and energy output*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Energy Stability** | Self-report (1-10) · Energy journal · CGM correlation | Crashes vs steady energy — how fuel affects function |
| **Protein Intake** | Food tracker (MyFitnessPal, Cronometer) · Simple Y/N target check | Protein sufficiency — muscle maintenance fuel |
| **Fiber Intake** | Food tracker · Simple Y/N target check | Fiber adequacy — gut health and glucose response |
| **Hydration** | Self-report · Water tracking app · Urine color check | Fluid balance — affects energy, cognition, performance |

### Metabolic Health Markers
*Lab indicators of metabolic function*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Triglycerides** | Lab upload (bloodwork, standard lipid panel) | Blood fats — elevated indicates metabolic issues |
| **HDL** | Lab upload (bloodwork, standard lipid panel) | "Good" cholesterol — higher is protective |
| **TG/HDL Ratio** | Calculated from lipid panel | Insulin resistance proxy — should be under 2 |
| **hsCRP** | Lab upload (bloodwork, high-sensitivity CRP) | Systemic inflammation — chronic = accelerated aging |
| **ALT / AST** | Lab upload (bloodwork, liver panel) | Liver enzymes — fatty liver and liver stress |
| **GGT** | Lab upload (bloodwork) | Liver stress marker — sensitive metabolic indicator |
| **Uric Acid** | Lab upload (bloodwork) | Metabolic stress — elevated indicates dysfunction |

### Hormonal Health
*Endocrine system function*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Testosterone** | Lab upload (bloodwork, morning draw, total + free) | Sex hormone — affects energy, muscle, mood, metabolism |
| **Thyroid (TSH)** | Lab upload (bloodwork, thyroid panel) | Metabolic regulator — controls energy expenditure |
| **DHEA-S** | Lab upload (bloodwork) | Adrenal reserve — declines with age, vitality marker |

## RECOVERY

**Restorative System — Your restoration**

### Sleep Quantity
*How much sleep you get*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Sleep Duration** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) · Manual log | Total hours asleep — most people need 7-9 hours |
| **Time in Bed** | Sleep tracker (auto) · Manual log | Hours attempting sleep — includes awake time |
| **Sleep Efficiency** | Sleep tracker (calculated) · Duration ÷ Time in bed | % of bed time actually asleep — should be 85%+ |

### Sleep Quality
*How restorative your sleep is*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Deep Sleep** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) | Physical restoration phase — body repair happens here |
| **REM Sleep** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) | Cognitive restoration phase — memory consolidation |
| **Sleep Latency** | Sleep tracker · Self-report (minutes to fall asleep) | Time to fall asleep — should be 10-20 minutes |
| **Wake-ups** | Sleep tracker (auto) · Self-report (count) | Nighttime awakenings — fragmented sleep is poor sleep |
| **Sleep Score** | Apple Watch · Oura · Whoop (proprietary composite) | Overall sleep quality — single number summary |

### Sleep Consistency
*Regularity of sleep schedule*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Bedtime Consistency** | Sleep tracker (calculated) · Manual tracking | Same time each night — circadian rhythm alignment |
| **Wake Time Consistency** | Sleep tracker (calculated) · Manual tracking | Same time each morning — most important for rhythm |
| **Social Jet Lag** | Calculated (weekend vs weekday sleep midpoint) | Weekend schedule shift — like crossing time zones weekly |

### Autonomic Recovery
*Nervous system restoration*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Morning HRV** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) · Whoop (auto) · HRV app | Overnight recovery status — did you actually recover? |
| **HRV Trend** | Wearable 7-day or 30-day average | Recovery trajectory — rising is good, falling is concern |
| **Resting HR Trend** | Wearable trend analysis | Chronic load indicator — rising RHR = overreaching |
| **Respiratory Rate** | Apple Watch (auto) · Oura (auto) | Overnight breathing — elevated can indicate illness/stress |

### Stress Recovery
*Stress hormone regulation*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Cortisol (morning)** | Saliva test (at-home kit: DUTCH, etc.) · Lab upload | Morning cortisol spike — should be high on waking |
| **Cortisol (evening)** | Saliva test (at-home kit) · Lab upload | Evening cortisol — should be low for good sleep |

### Subjective Recovery
*How recovered you feel*

| Metric | Measurement Sources | What It Tells You |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Perceived Energy** | Morning check-in (1-10) | How rested you feel — subjective recovery signal |
| **Readiness** | Oura/Whoop readiness score · Self-assessment (1-10) | Ready to perform or need rest? — training guidance |
| **Soreness** | Self-report (1-10) | Muscular recovery — DOMS and tissue repair status |
| **Motivation** | Self-report (1-10) | Mental recovery — low motivation can signal overtraining |
